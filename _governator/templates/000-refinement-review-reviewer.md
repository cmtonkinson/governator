# Task 000: Refinement Review (Reviewer)

## Objective
Validate that refinement outputs are aligned and internally consistent.

## Inputs
- `GOVERNATOR.md`
- Architecture docs under `_governator/docs/`
- Milestones (`_governator/docs/milestones.md`)
- Epics (`_governator/docs/epics.md`)
- Refinement summary and transcript paths appended to this task

## Output
In your `review.json` comments, be exhaustive. Confirm that:
- Requirements, constraints, and assumptions are consistent across artifacts.
- Milestones and epics align with the stated intent.
- No contradictions were introduced during refinement.

If inconsistencies remain, reject the refinement and explain what must change.
